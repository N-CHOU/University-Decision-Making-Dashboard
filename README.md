University Dashboard: Global & Singapore Education Insights
Overview
Welcome to the University Dashboard project! This interactive Tableau dashboard provides comprehensive insights into higher education institutions worldwide, with a special focus on Singapore. Whether you're a student planning your education journey, a parent evaluating options, or an education professional analyzing trends, this dashboard makes complex university data accessible and easy to understand.
What Does This Dashboard Show?
Our dashboard brings together critical information about universities that helps answer questions like:

How much will my education really cost? View tuition fees alongside other education-related expenses
What's the cost of living? Compare living expenses across different cities and countries
How prestigious is the university? Check QS World University Rankings
What are my career prospects? Review employability scores to understand job market outcomes
Singapore vs. Global: See how Singapore universities compare internationally

Data Categories Explained
Cost of Education
The total tuition and mandatory fees charged by universities, typically shown per academic year. This varies by program, degree level, and institution.
Non-Tuition Education Costs
Additional expenses required for education including textbooks, course materials, lab fees, technology requirements, and student services fees.
Cost of Living Index
A standardized measure comparing how expensive it is to live in different cities. A higher index means higher costs for housing, food, transportation, and daily expenses.
Employability Scores
Ratings that reflect how well graduates from a university perform in the job market, based on factors like employment rate, salary levels, and employer reputation.
QS World University Rankings
An internationally recognized ranking system that evaluates universities on academic reputation, employer reputation, faculty quality, research output, and internationalization.
